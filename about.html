About

Hands-on growth and AI automation partner.

I help B2B founders and marketing leaders close the loop between ads, CRM, and sales — then layer AI agents on top so you can scale without more headcount.

Quick snapshot

7+ years running paid, lifecycle, and CRM for B2B SaaS and agencies. Now helping a small handful of teams build systems that compound.

  • Performance & growth strategy across Google, Meta, and LinkedIn
  • Revenue-first tracking across HubSpot, GA4, and offline conversions
  • AI agents, workflows, and copilots to automate the repeatable

From “more leads” to revenue systems.

I started in growth working inside scrappy B2B teams — where every euro spent had to earn its keep. Running ads was never just about traffic. It was about: how do we get this into HubSpot, sync it with actual pipeline, then figure out what to double down on?

Since then, I’ve led growth and performance for SaaS products, agencies, and consulting offers. I’ve managed budgets from a few thousand per month to six figures per quarter, across Google, Meta, LinkedIn, and niche channels.

What I care about is building systems: clear positioning, consistent acquisition, and clean data so you can make decisions without guessing.

Where AI fits in.

AI isn’t a magic lever — but it is a force multiplier when you already have the basics in place.

I help teams use AI agents and copilots to:

  • Qualify and route leads faster
  • Automate routine follow-ups and hand-offs
  • Enrich and clean CRM data automatically
  • Give sales and marketing a single, reliable view of the funnel

The goal isn’t to replace your team. It’s to remove low-leverage work so they can focus on the conversations and decisions that actually move revenue.

How I like to work.

I keep things intentionally lean: no bloated retainers, no 40-slide decks that never ship.

Instead, we’ll usually:

  • Audit your current funnel, tracking, and campaigns
  • Define the 1–2 core growth motions that matter most
  • Set up tracking so we can tie efforts to revenue
  • Layer in AI workflows where they unlock clear leverage

I work best with founders and marketing leaders who want a partner that’s hands-on, direct, and focused on measurable outcomes.

If that sounds like you, we’ll probably get along.
